<h1 align="center" id="title">Shopping-Cart Application</h1>



<p id="description">The Shopping Cart Application is an intuitive web-based project designed to simulate a real-world e-commerce platform. Built using React.js it enables users to explore a variety of products add them to their shopping cart and manage their selections with ease. This application incorporates advanced features like dynamic price calculation discount savings and responsive design to enhance user experience. 

Key highlights include: 
<ul>
<li>Adding and removing products from the cart with quantity management.</li> <li>Real-time updates of the subtotal discounts and total cost.</li> <li>Predefined promotional rules for discounts on specific items (e.g. discounts on Cheese Soup and Butter).</li></ul></p>
